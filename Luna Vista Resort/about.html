Welcome to Luna Vista Resort

Luna Vista Resort is a relaxing beachfront destination located in Station 2, Boracay. Known for its beautiful ocean views and vibrant atmosphere, the resort offers a perfect tropical escape for families, couples, and friends.

Guests enjoy modern amenities including an infinity pool, spa, restaurant dining, and comfortable accommodations just steps away from the famous white sand beach and D'Mall.

About Luna Vista

Located in the heart of Boracay, Luna Vista Resort provides a memorable island experience with affordable luxury. With over 400 rooms, modern facilities, and a lively beachfront atmosphere, guests can relax, explore the island, and enjoy unforgettable sunsets by the sea.
