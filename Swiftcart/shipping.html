Packaging
  • All orders are packed in recycled cardboard or eco-poly mailers
  • Fragile items include extra bubble wrap and "FRAGILE" stickers
  • No plastic fillers — we use shredded paper or cornstarch peanuts
  • Gift wrapping available at checkout for ₱49
Tracking
  • Tracking number sent via email and SMS once shipped
  • Real-time updates every time your package moves
  • Visit our Track Order page anytime
  • Delivery photo provided by courier upon drop-off
⚠️ Important Notes
  • Delivery times are business days (Mon–Sat, excluding holidays)
  • Orders placed after 3PM are processed the next business day
  • Ensure someone is available to receive; 3 attempts are made
  • After 3 failed attempts, package is returned to our warehouse
International
  • Customs duties and import taxes are the buyer's responsibility
  • We declare accurate values — no false "gift" declarations
  • Some items (batteries, liquids) cannot be shipped internationally
  • Full country list available at checkout
