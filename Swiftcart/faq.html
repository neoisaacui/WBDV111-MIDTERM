Help Center

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find quick answers to the most common questions about ordering, shipping, returns, and your account.

Orders
How do I place an order?▾
Browse products, click "Add to Cart", then open your cart and click "Checkout Now". You'll confirm your address and payment before your order is placed. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately after.
Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?▾
You can modify or cancel an order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, the order enters our fulfillment process and changes may not be possible. Contact our support team as quickly as possible if you need to make changes.
How do I track my order?▾
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also visit our Track Order page and enter your order ID to see real-time updates.
What happens if an item is out of stock after I order?▾
We'll notify you immediately and offer either a full refund or a substitute product. We never silently ship a different item without your approval.
Shipping
How long does delivery take?▾
Metro Manila: 1–2 business days. Luzon provinces: 2–4 days. Visayas & Mindanao: 3–6 days. International orders: 7–14 days. Exact estimates are shown at checkout based on your address.
Is there free shipping?▾
Yes! Free standard shipping on all orders over ₱999 within the Philippines. SwiftCart Pro members get free shipping on every order with no minimum. International shipping fees vary by country.
Do you ship internationally?▾
Yes, we ship to 40+ countries. International shipping is calculated at checkout. Some products may have restrictions due to local import regulations. See our Shipping Policy for a full list of countries.
What carriers do you use?▾
We partner with LBC, J&T Express, Ninja Van, and DHL for domestic orders. International orders are shipped via DHL Express or FedEx depending on destination.
Returns & Refunds
What is your return policy?▾
We offer a 30-day no-questions-asked return policy on most items. Products must be in original condition with tags attached. Items marked as "Final Sale" are not eligible for returns. See our full Returns Policy.
How do I start a return?▾
Go to your Account → Orders → select the order → click "Return Item". Choose your reason, print the prepaid return label, and drop it off at any accredited courier branch. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days after we receive the item.
How long does a refund take?▾
Once we receive and inspect your return (1–2 days), refunds are issued within 3–5 business days to your original payment method. Credit card refunds may take an additional 3–7 days to appear on your statement.
What if I received a damaged or wrong item?▾
We're so sorry! Take a photo of the item and contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We'll arrange a free replacement or full refund — your choice. No need to return the item in this case.
Account
How do I create an account?▾
Click "Sign In" on the homepage and then "Create one free". You can register with your email or continue with Google. Creating an account lets you track orders, save addresses, and earn SwiftPoints rewards.
I forgot my password. What do I do?▾
Click "Forgot password?" on the sign-in page, enter your email, and we'll send a reset link within 2 minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it. The link expires after 30 minutes.
How do I delete my account?▾
Go to Account Settings → Privacy → Delete Account. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days per our Privacy Policy. Pending orders must be resolved before deletion.
Payment
What payment methods do you accept?▾
We accept Visa, Mastercard, GCash, Maya, GrabPay, bank transfer, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for eligible orders within Metro Manila. All online payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.
Is it safe to enter my card details?▾
Absolutely. We use bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption and are PCI DSS compliant. We never store your full card number — payments are processed directly through our certified payment partners.
Can I pay in installments?▾
Yes! We offer 0% installment plans (3, 6, or 12 months) on orders over ₱3,000 with participating Visa and Mastercard credit cards. Select "Installment" at checkout to see available plans.
